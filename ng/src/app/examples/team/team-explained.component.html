How it works

Everything here is standard Angular, and it talks with DNN and 2sxc to get data.

  • the menu showing the business units uses the SxcApp.data from 2sxc to get all items of a specific type (in this case the BusinessUnits)
  • if you click on a business unit, the route is changed
  • the list showing the team uses SxcApp.query to query the Persons based on the selected BusinessUnit
  • if you're logged in as admin, the sxc-toolbar attribute gives you mouse-over menus to edit/delete things
